Site Plan

Yuma Natalia Hanane

Overview

Name

Modestly

This name represents a platform that shows the beauty of modesty.

Purpose

The site presents an argumentative text on modesty, an album of pictures of people dressed modestly, and a form through which the readers can send their feedback.

Scenarios

The site will answer to the following questions:

  • - Can one be beautiful and stylish while being decent?
  • - Where can I share my feedback?
  • - Can my pictures be posted on the site?

Color Schema

The text color on the navigation and footer background color will be #f7f4ee, the navigation and footer will be #01463c, the background color for dialog boxes will be #effcf9

Typography

The body will be Opens Sans

For example: Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Totam ipsum unde tempora qui minus eligendi, quisquam natus dicta repellendus aliquam.

The navigation and headings will be Crimson Text

For example: Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Quo cupiditate officia atque natus, a magni tempora saepe eveniet dignissimos deserunt.
